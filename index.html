Beef Flank Steak

Beef flank steak is cut of meat from the cow's abdominal muscle. It is extremely lean and muscular with very little fat but carries an intense, rich beefy flavor. Flank steak is a relatively long, flat cut of beef, about a foot long, less than one-inch thick with very distinct grains.
